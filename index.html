Hi, I'm Gautam.

I'm a Deep Learning Software Engineer, currently working at Qualcomm India. I'm working on the development of Snapdragon Neural Processing Engine, which enables the execution of deep neural networks on mobile devices. Prior to that, I worked as a Chipset Power Systems Engineer at Qualcomm. I was responsible for driving the roadmap for power management features (Hardware and Software) in Snapdragon processors, namely SDM415 and SDM632 processors. Apart from that, I also worked on development and exploration of machine learning based solutions for improving the power and performance of chipsets..

I earned my undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee in 2015. My research interests broadly lie in the field of Deep Learning, System Architecture, Algorithms, Data Structures and Low Power Systems.
